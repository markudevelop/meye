Welcome to Meye

Meye privately records your screen & audio on this Mac, so you can search and chat with everything you've seen. Nothing leaves your computer.

Turn on recording

Meye runs a small background recorder. macOS will ask you to allow Screen Recording and Microphone — that's how Meye sees and hears.

Connect an AI model

Chat & automations need an AI model. When you're ready, open Settings → AI & Advanced and add your own API key or a free local model (Ollama). You can do this anytime — it's not required to start recording.

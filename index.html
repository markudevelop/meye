Performance & privacy

Balance battery life against detail, control your privacy, and turn on voice commands.

Power profile
Lower = lighter on battery and memory. Higher = more detail captured.
Capture sources
Choose what Meye records. Turn off anything you don't want captured.
🖥 Screen (video)
Captures your screen for Search & Timeline. Turning off removes the macOS recording dot.
🎙 Microphone
Records & transcribes your microphone.
🔊 Computer audio
Records sound coming out of your Mac (calls, videos). On a call this captures the other people too — mind their consent.
Audio accuracy
Better transcription uses more memory. (Only matters if an audio source is on.)
Discreet mode
Hides Meye's dock & tray icons during screen shares. Closing the window fully quits Meye (recording keeps running — reopen from Applications). The macOS recording dot stays, so people you share with are still informed.
Voice button (push-to-talk)
Shows a floating mic button. Tap it, then say pause, resume, new chat, open timeline, or search for … ("Hey Meye" optional). On-demand only — nothing listens in the background. Needs Audio on; recognition is a touch slower in Balanced/Saver (batch mode).

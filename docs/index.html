FAQ

Good questions

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. Meye records, indexes, and runs AI search entirely on your own Mac. Nothing is uploaded — there are no accounts and no servers involved in capturing or searching your memory.

What does Pro unlock?

Pro funds development and gives you priority updates plus advanced periodic summaries. After checkout you'll receive a license key by email to activate Pro in the app.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro is a monthly subscription you can cancel at any time from the Stripe billing portal — no questions asked. Meye stays free to use either way.

Which platforms are supported?

Meye is a native macOS app today. It builds on screenpipe and keeps the recorder alive with a launchd background agent.
